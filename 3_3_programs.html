Tetris

Tetris is a a classic game of arranging pieces that consists of different arragements of four squares to complete rows before the screen floods. Collaboration with Seph Xiao.

Instructions: Press left/right arrows to move pieces.
Press up/down to rotate pieces.
Press "P" to pause game.
Press "R" to reset game.

H Fractal

H Fractals is a program meant to emphasize the power of recursion codes. While recursion is not as efficient or "debuggable", its code has an elegance that surpasses that of other types of loops like "for" loops.

Instructions: Press up/down to change fractal level.

Dots

Dots is a classic territorial game that requires strategy and some luck to draw out the most boxes.

Instructions: Press left and right arrows to move pieces.

Fancy Wheels

Fancy wheels is a unique program that creates a unique pattern of color gradients, rotation sequences, and geometric figures.

Instructions: Press up/down arrows to change number of wheels.

Othello

Othello (or Reversi) is a classical game to cover the most space possible by trapping the other player's pieces to capture his spots.

Instructions: Press legal spot to place respective pieces.
