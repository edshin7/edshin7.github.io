Portable Laptop Stand (desktop)

Technologies used:
Solidworks, Inkscape, Rabbit Laser Cutter, LaserCut 5.3

Materials used:
1/8" Acrylic (transparent), 1/4" Acrylic (black)

Summary:
This is my custom-designed portable laptop stand. This is designed for ease on body, portability, and cost-efficiency. Not only can this be compressed into a 9" by 9" by 1/2" object, it also comes in two elevated heights at roughly 6" and 9". This makes for an excellent substitute for expensive and stationary computer desks.
