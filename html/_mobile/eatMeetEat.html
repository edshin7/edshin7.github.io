Eat Meet Eat

Technologies used:
Swift, CoreData, CoreLocation, MapKit, Zomato API, Xcode

Summary:
A mobile web application that chooses a restaurant with a single button press for hungry users. Upon chosing a restaurant, the app accesses the Zomato API to provide information, including reviews, menus, and backgrounds.
