Verto Animo

Technologies used:
Unity3D, C#, MonoDevelop, Audacity, Photoshop

Summary:
Verto Animo is a 2-player coop game that my team and I worked on for Carnegie Mellon University's game organization, the Game Creation Society.

The game was built on the ideas of teamwork and identity. The player's controls are deliberately scrambled so that the players would be encouraged to communicate with each other in order to progress and succeed.
