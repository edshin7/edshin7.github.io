First-Person Monument Valley

Technologies used:
HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, jQuery, Three.js, WebGL

Summary:
Created a first-person simulation of the popular game, Monument Valley 2. Experimented with the user’s experience with a game mechanic from Monument Valley 2. User experiences new worlds and gradually enriching environments to encourage further exploration of an “impossible” world.
