Pizza Kitchen VR

Technologies used:
Unity3D, C#, Visual Studio, VR, Oculus Quest 2

Summary:
Pizza Kitchen VR is a virtual reality game that simulates the process of cooking a pizza. This is designed to treat players with ADHD by encouraging multitasking in a "busy" environment

The game consists of several steps to making a pizza First, players are prompted to make pizzas according to the upcoming orders. Afterwards, they would take them to an oven and bake them without undercooking or overcooking them. Lastly players would deliver them to a designated area. Players will be challenged to complete this task as quickly and accurately as possible.
