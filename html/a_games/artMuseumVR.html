Art Museum VR

Technologies used:
Unity3D, C#, Visual Studio, VR, Oculus Quest 2

Summary:
Art Museum VR is a virtual musuem simulator designed to efficiently teach users about abstract art through VR. In the simulator, players go through a repeated process of examining the artwork and their respective information and answering questions to progress.

This game was developed for the thesis, "Virtual Reality vs the Onscreen Experience: Gauging the Strengths of Digital Learning." This was compared to a tablet iteration in order determine whether VR is better than the onscreen methods to teach and learn and find the pros and cons from each experience.
