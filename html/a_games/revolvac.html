Revolvac

Technologies used:
Unity3D, C#, Android, FireAlpaca, Piskel

Summary:
Revolvac is a block puzzle, in which the player must match lines of blocks on a 9X9 grid to score points and avoid a game over. Play with three different rotation modes. Feel the tension with a timer. Relax without a timer. Challenge yourself in multiple ways.
