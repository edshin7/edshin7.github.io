Portable Laptop Stand

This is my custom-designed portable laptop stand. This is designed for ease on body, portability, and cost-efficiency. Not only can this be compressed into a 9" by 9" by 3/8" object, it also comes in two elevated heights at roughly 6" and 9". This makes for an excellent substitute for expensive and stationary computer desks.

Tools: Laser Cutting, Solidworks, Inkscape

Materials: 1/8" Acrylic (clear, colored)

Marble Climber

This is a replication of an existing marble climber. Fascinated by the idea of an automaton moving a detached object, I created a series of Solidworks parts and printed a 3D printed version, which the marble climber lacks as it is commonly made of wood.

Tools: Laser Cutting, 3D printer, Solidworks

Materials: ABS plastic
