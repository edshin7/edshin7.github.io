Realms and Bridges

An inspiration of the game, Monument Valley, this game essentially creates a first-person view of Ida.

The objective of this project was to create and explore a world that allows a free range of movement and perspective to interpret the environment in ways not possible from normal right-side up standpoints.

The player will have the ability to change the camera's direction and angle to explore the worlds and progress towards the goal, which will be randomly reset and generate a new environment to traverse. The idea is to keep the player moving as each round will generate a different world and provide more color to bring more life into the game.

Language: JavaScript
Tools: three.js

Play Game: Click Me

Roman Arcs

Roman Arcs is a type of digital clock that takes the rules of Roman numerals and reinterprets them as a code in the form of arcs.

Center arc = I            Last arc = L
Second arc = V         Center red arc = Subtracting I
Third arc = X             Third red arc = Subtracting X

Tool(s): p5.js

Rain Portrait

Rain Portrait is a program that paints a portrait with different colored ripples. From the inspiration of a rainy day, the codes were written with the idea of a reflection of person's face being distorted and abstracted by the rain drops falling and rippling in a puddle.

Instructions: Move the mouse around to paint the portrait.
Click to reset the program. (The ripples stop at 4000 to prevent computer crashes)

Triangle Hand Clock

Triangle Hand Clock is simply a clock that uses a continuously changing triangle to tell time. The idea was to create an abstract clock that tells time in an unorthodox way. Each corner of the clock represents three basic units of time: seconds, minutes, and hours. As time moves, the triangle would change form. Its color changes every minute. The idea was to create a relationship where "time" would affect "space".

Instructions: Press "T" to show/hide time units.

Tool(s): p5.js

Click here to play: Triangle Hand Clock
