Key Ripple

Key Ripple is an endeaver of sorts to create a more fun and engaging way of interacting with the computer and its keyboard. As this is a simple step towards such goal, the hope is that a newer idea would be implemented so the computers can be viewed as more than just a functional machine for mundane tasks.

Instructions: Press any letter key to create ripples and other special effects.

Click here: Key Ripple

Rain Portrait

Rain Portrait is a program that paints a portrait with different colored ripples. From the inspiration of a rainy day, the codes were written with the idea of a reflection of person's face being distorted and abstracted by the rain drops falling and rippling in a puddle.

Instructions: Move the mouse around to paint the portrait.
Click to reset the program. (The ripples stop at 4000 to prevent computer crashes)

Click here: Rain Portrait

Rolls and Struggles

Rolls and Struggles creates a scenery transition by using a conveyer belt. A series of balls are created at random times to make that impression. In order to make the program more engaging, the idea of the balls rolling against the belt's movement was implemented.

Instructions: None

Click here: Rolls and Struggles

Dancing Stars

Dancing Stars is a fun program that uses curves. Astroid was selected to create a star theme, as the Greek root "astron" means star. The second theme, "dancing", was chosen to create a playful and mysterious feel to the two astroids made of white dots as though the astroids were constellations dancing about in the night sky.

Instructions: Move the mouse around to change size and position.
Press 1 for normal mode.
Press 2 for blur.
Press 3 for imprinting.

Click here: Dancing Stars

Triangle Hand Clock

Triangle Hand Clock is simply a clock that uses a continuously changing triangle to tell time. The idea was to create an abstract clock that tells time in an unorthodox way. Each corner of the clock represents three basic units of time: seconds, minutes, and hours. As time moves, the triangle would change form. Its color changes every minute. The idea was to create a relationship where "time" would affect "space".

Instructions: Press "T" to show/hide time units.

Click here: Triangle Hand Clock
