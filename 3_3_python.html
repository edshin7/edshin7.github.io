Edge 2D

Edge 2D is a two-dimensional version of an iPhone game called Edge, by Mobigames. Although the 2D world is not the same as a 3D one, Edge 2D holds most of the experiences the player gets from playing it. Rather than a matter of increasing diffculty, however, Edge 2D attempts to increase its playfulness as the player progresses.

Instructions: Refer to help screen for details.

H Fractal

H Fractals is a program meant to emphasize the power of recursion codes. While recursion is not as efficient or "debuggable", it has an elegance surpasses that of other types of loops like "for" loops.

Instructions: Press up/down to change fractal level.

Tetris

Tetris is a a classic game of arranging pieces that consists of different arragements of four squares to complete rows before the screen floods. Collaboration with Seph Xiao.

Instructions: Press left/right arrows to move pieces.
Press up/down to rotate pieces.
Press "P" to pause game.
Press "R" to reset game.
